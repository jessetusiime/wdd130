White Water Rafting

The World's Best Water Rafting Company. We offer the best rafting services in the country while giving our clients the best thrilling experience on our fast and rapid waters. Come get the unforgetable experience with our experienced and highly qualified staff.

History

White Water Rafting was established in 2005 by Van de Guul, a man from Holland. Guul holds a Bachelors Degree in tourism and hospiality and he came to Uganda in the year 2003. He then went on to establish White Water Rafting in 2005.

White Water Rafting ranks as the best rafting company in Uganda due to its exceptional services and well trained tour guides. It has also contributed greatly to the tourism sector due to the high number of tourists who love rafting.
