Rapid Rocky Adventure

This is an exciting fast paced rafting activity done the rocky river while maintaining control and composure as you explore the coral reefs. The route is very rocky and thrilling and therefore requires rafters who have good experience.

High Wave Tour

This activity also requires rafters to encounter high and aggressive water waves that can be thrilling and scary bit the experience is worth it. Have the ultimate thrilling experience as you manouver the vigorous waves.

Downriver Rafting

This involves rafters going down the river while exploring the Basinian mountains and Daun river banks. The route has great scenary and is ideal for taking photos because it is smoother and more calm. Other activities on this route include Kayaking.
